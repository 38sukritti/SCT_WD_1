Our Adoption Process

1

Browse Available Pets

View our pets online or visit our shelter to meet them in person. Our staff can help you find the perfect match for your lifestyle.

2

Submit Application

Complete our adoption application form either online or at our facility. This helps us understand your home environment and preferences.

3

Meet & Greet

Schedule a time to meet your potential new pet. For dogs, we may require multiple visits or a home visit to ensure compatibility.

4

Home Preparation

Prepare your home with all the essentials before bringing your new pet home. We'll provide a checklist of recommended supplies.

5

Finalize Adoption

Complete the paperwork, pay the adoption fee, and take your new family member home! We're always here for post-adoption support.

Processing Time

Most applications are processed within 2-3 business days. Complex cases may take longer.

Requirements

You must be 18+ with valid ID. We verify living situations and may require landlord approval.

Trial Period

All adoptions include a 14-day trial period to ensure the pet is a good fit for your home.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your adoption fees?

Our adoption fees vary by animal type and age:

  • Puppies (under 1 year): $250-$350
  • Adult dogs: $150-$250
  • Kittens (under 1 year): $125-$175
  • Adult cats: $75-$125
  • Small animals: $25-$75

All fees include spay/neuter, vaccinations, microchipping, and a health check.

What is the adoption process?

Our process includes these steps:

  1. Complete an adoption application (online or in-person)
  2. Meet with an adoption counselor to discuss your needs
  3. Meet potential pets that match your lifestyle
  4. For dogs, we may require a home visit
  5. Finalize paperwork and pay adoption fee

The entire process typically takes 3-7 days.

Can I return a pet if it doesn't work out?

Yes, we have a lifetime return policy. If for any reason you can no longer care for your adopted pet, we require that you return them to us rather than rehoming them yourself.

We offer a 14-day trial period where you can return the pet for a full refund of the adoption fee if it's not a good fit.

Do you have a trial period?

Yes! We offer a 14-day trial period for all adoptions. During this time:

  • You can return the pet for any reason
  • You'll receive a full refund of the adoption fee
  • Our staff is available for support and advice

This gives you time to ensure the pet is a good fit for your home and lifestyle.

What should I bring to the adoption appointment?

Please bring:

  • Valid photo ID
  • Proof of address (utility bill, lease agreement)
  • If renting, proof that pets are allowed
  • All family members who will live with the pet
  • Any current dogs (for meet-and-greet if adopting another dog)
